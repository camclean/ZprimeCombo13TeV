Statistics HATS@LPC Exercises¶

External links:
  • Indico Agenda of Statistics HATS@LPC 2014.
  • documentation of theta and theta-auto http://theta-framework.org/
The exercises should be done in the following order:
  • Day 1 (Hypothesis Tests): After the Technical Setup, go on to Exercise 1: Hypothesis tests and p-values
  • Day 2 (Intervals and Limits): Exercise 2: (Modified) Frequentist Limits and Exercise 3: Bayesian Inference
  • Day 3 (Advanced Models): Exercise 4: Real Analysis (theta) and Tprime tutorial

If you completed all exercises of the given day before the time is up, feel free to look at Bonus Questions for more.

Related exercises using the RooStats-based “combine” tool can be found on the CMSDAS Statistics page.
